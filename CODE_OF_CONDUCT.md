# Code of Conduct

Fleet is an open source project for builders collaborating in public. Keep
discussion technical, specific, and respectful.

Expected behavior:

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Assume good intent, but ask for evidence when technical claims are unclear.
- Critique code, designs, and decisions, not people.
- Keep issues and PRs focused on reproducible behavior and concrete outcomes.
- Do not post private credentials, certificates, tokens, or personal data.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, hate speech, sustained
personal attacks, or publishing someone else's private information.

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and may block accounts
that make collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
